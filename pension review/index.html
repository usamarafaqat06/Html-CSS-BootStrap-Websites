about us

how we get paid

As a firm dedicated to providing an honest and impartial service to our clients, no matter what their needs, we think it is important to be completely transparent on how we get paid.

our fee

You'll be pleased to know that our fees come directly from the Financial Advisors doing the reviews and not from you or your pension pot.

We do not charge the client a fee at all. We get paid by the Financial Advisors for organising the reviews for them. For every Review we generate, we get a Referral Fee making this service completely free of charge for you, the client

free pension review

We will take a detailed look into your Pension Scheme to make sure there is nothing preventing your Pension from performing better, maximising your returns.

This may mean leaving it as it is, or a recommendation to transfer. In either case you are not obliged to act on any recommendations. Your Pension Review will still be free and you are under no obligation to take advantage of any more services.

our services

reviewing your pension

Whether you’re planning well ahead and want to invest in the right pension, or are approaching retirement and want to secure the best possible annuity, we provide our clients with the best possible free pension advice so they can make appropriate financial decisions based on what is right for them and their families.

The advice is both technically accurate and independent, which means you can be sure that our recommendations are in your interests, and no one else’s.

But before any recommendations are offered, we always start with an assessment of your current situation, to see whether you would benefit from taking out a new pension or transferring your existing one to another provider. This involves an initial free pension review, which we can do over the phone and won’t take very long.

If we think we can be of help, and in the majority of cases the people we speak to would benefit from making a pension switch, then one of our free pension advisors will carry out a more comprehensive analysis.

transffering pensions for a better retirement

Unfortunately, many people never get around to doing this because they believe they’re locked into their existing pension, especially if they have had it for a while. Or they think that changing their pension provider will be a long-winded and difficult process that isn’t worth bothering with.

In fact, with the right help and advice, transferring pensions doesn’t have to be either time-consuming or complicated. That means it’s perfectly possible for you to escape from an expensive or poorly performing pension and take of advantage of much better deals that are now on offer. ​
